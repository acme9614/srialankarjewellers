Our Story

Founded over a century ago in Ranchi, Sri Alankar Jewellers has been synonymous with timeless craftsmanship and luxurious designs. Our legacy is built on the art of combining traditional techniques with modern innovation.

From exquisite bridal sets to everyday masterpieces, our commitment is to create jewelry that not only adorns you but also connects you with a rich cultural legacy.
